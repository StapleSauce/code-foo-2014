In order to determine how many gamers are in the San Francisco Bay Area, I wanted to determine what would allow someone the potential to invest in gaming as a hobby. I used this information from the Bureau of Labor statistics. People Employed in the San Francisco Bay Area

  • 2,049,560 (San Francisco-Oakland-Fremont, CA)
  • 930,070 (San Jose-Sunnyvale-Santa Clara, CA)
  • San Francisco-Oakland-Fremont and San Jose-Sunnyvale-Santa Clara combined 2,979,630

Therefore I believe that 2,979,630 people have the potential to be gamers.
